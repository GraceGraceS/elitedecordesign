About us

Elite Decor is a design and merchandising company located in India. We work on commercial spaces, project management, architectural critique and more.

We are a team of planners,architect,landscape architects and interior designers.We provide a range of work covering brand identity,art direction,product design,interior design,custom furniture and fabrication.

We offers a variety of services from interior design to architectural visualization. We have close relationships with big name companies located around the world. What's great is that we accept input from our clients and learn from it.
